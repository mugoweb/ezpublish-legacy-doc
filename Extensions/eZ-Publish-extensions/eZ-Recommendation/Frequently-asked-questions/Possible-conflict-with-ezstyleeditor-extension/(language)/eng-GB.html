Possible conflict with ezstyleeditor extension

Possible conflict with ezstyleeditor extension

Question: Tracking does not seem to work in my eZ Publish installation although I followed the installation guide and implemented tracking in the pagelayout.tpl.

Answer: There seems to be a conflict with the ezstyleeditor extension. Try to disable the extension and check the debug.log for click events.
